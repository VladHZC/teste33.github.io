Welcome To Skatehive

What’s Skatehive

SkateHive is based on the anti-establishment roots of skateboarding, and is working at taking back control of our activities from the social media giants, who profit off the monopoly of our content.

SkateHive is build on top of decentralized platforms that allows us to own both our accounts, and our communities, which means rather than advertising on our content or selling users meta-data to generate value, we tokenize the likes, allowing us to retain the value generated by our content, for it to be sold on the free market in the form of a cryptocurrency.

This has allowed this humble community to self fund huge online Games of S.K.A.T.E, giveaways, rewards for contests, events, and even help skaters buy new equipment! Everyone is welcome in this community, so long as you are respectful to others.

If you need help understanding more about Skatehive, feel free to ask for help in our Discord, and one of the many longtime SkateHive members will help you out! Click on the invite image bellow and join us.

How everything started

SkateHive began simply as a community of skateboarders and has evolved into much more! We are people from all walks of life. We are seeking evolution and revolution in the world of action/extreme sports.

At SkateHive we learn from eachother, support eachother, and earn crypto together in a system that we have created and voluntarily choose to participate in. What we are pushing for is finacial independence, liberty, and absolute ownership over ourselves and our data.

Introducing Odysee and Peakd

Skatehive community utilizes 2 decentralized platforms for content sharing, Odysee and Peakd.

You are about to find out why we recommend Odysee and Peakd when it comes to content sharing and how you can utilize both platforms and earn 2 different cryptocurrencies (LBC & Hive) for the same content you already share on other social media platforms for free.

What’s Odysee

Odysee (https://odysee.com/) is a video content sharing platform in which you own your own content. This means that no one can delete it.

Content creators on Odysee earn LBC (cryptocurrency) according to their video views so the more views your video gets, the more LBC you earn.

There’s also some Rewards for beginners to start getting used to it, so give it a try!

What’s Peakd

Peakd (https://peakd.com/) is a blogging platform powering communities the same way Reddit does and Blogspots used to do.

What makes Peakd unique is that you are in control of your content and no one can delete it, just like Odysee (THANKS BLOCKCHAIN)

Users on Peakd subscribe to their favourite communities (just like Skatehive), engage with each other, share content and get rewarded with Hive (cryptocurrency) for their actions: liking and commenting, for instance.

The Synergy Between Odysee & Peakd

As mentioned Odysee is a video sharing platform, Peakd is a blogging platform and both of them are censorship free.

You can share your videos in the first and embed it on your blog post in the second and BOOM

You earned twice for the same content you would only get likes and maybe a share on Instagram.

Why Odysee & Peakd?

Because we want to take back control of our content and data

We are the “products” in centralized social media. Big internet platforms like Instagram, Youtube and Facebook might be “free” to use but they are making millions from selling our data and content.

Have you ever watched The Great Hack or Social Dilemma?

We are not “their” tools and money-making machines

Decentralized platforms like Odysee and Peakd don’t collect our data and allow us to earn cryptocurrency for our content thanks to their decentralized nature and awesome tech.

In other words, Odysee and Peakd are our tools instead of us being the tools in centralized social media (Instagram, Youtube and Facebook).

You can either sell that cryptocurrency for your local currency or keep it in your Odysee and Peakd wallets so you can support other skateboarders.
